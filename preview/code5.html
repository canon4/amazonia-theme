Product History

The art of Tucumã weaving is a sacred tradition passed from mothers to daughters for over five centuries. This specific pattern, known as the "Rainforest Labyrinth," represents the interconnectedness of all living spirits in the Amazon.

The process begins with the sustainable harvest of Tucumã palm leaves during the waxing moon, a practice believed to ensure the strength and longevity of the fibers.
